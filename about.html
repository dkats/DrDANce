Who am I: My name is Dan Kats, and I'm a medical student. During middle school, high school, and college, I didn't exercise at all, because I always made the excuse that I "didn't have time". However, when I got into med school, I decided that I couldn't tell my patients to exercise if I didn't do it myself. My classmates and I started going to cycling classes, and I quickly became obsessed with being active.

Who is this for? Children and parents, who want to become more active, but do not have time or ways to workout. This website is meant to help you exercise wherever and whenever in fun and engaging ways. Exercise is incredibly important for kids' health, so a great way for parents to help their kids grow is to teach them to be active. By exercising together, parents/guardians and their kids will not only be more health, but will also have more bonding time (without bright screens between them)!

How should this be used? On this website, you'll find lots of ideas for how to exercise at home, or wherever you are. Everything is completely free and available for download.

Where did the idea come from? Being overweight leads to a much higher chance of disease. Exercise is a great and easy way to lose weight, boost your health, and keep yourself as healthy as possible. From my own experiences, I know that it's difficult to find the time, motivation, and equipment necessary to exercise, so I wanted to put this website together as a resource for working out at home for any amount of time.
